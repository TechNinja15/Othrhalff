# Welcome to the Othrhalff Wiki

A modern campus-based anonymous social platform designed to help students connect, interact, and express themselves freely. Othrhalff bridges the gap between anonymity and meaningful social connections through innovative features built specifically for the Gen Z college demographic.

---

## 🌟 Key Pillars

### 💬 Anonymous Live Feed
Share thoughts, confessions, ideas, and opinions with your campus community without the fear of judgment or social anxiety. 
- **Freedom of Expression**: Post completely anonymously to the campus feed.
- **Polls & Comments**: Interact with posts, vote in campus-wide polls, and comment under threads anonymously.
- **Local Reach**: Designed to prioritize content originating within your university.

### 👻 Gradual Profile Reveal (Ghost Chemistry Match)
A gamified matching system that builds trust and excitement by gradually unblurring user profiles as interaction deepens.
- **Identity Protection**: Match profiles start completely blurred.
- **Progressive Disclosure**: As you chat and exchange messages, profiles gradually reveal details and unblur.
- **Chemistry Meter**: Track interaction milestones to unlock full profile details.

### ⚡ Sparx Hub
The pulse of the campus, combined in a single interactive dashboard.
- **Geolocated Campus Heatmap**: View a dynamic visual map of live activity hotspots across the university.
- **Vertical Video Stories**: Watch and share vertical, TikTok-style daily updates and stories with your peers.
- **In-App Camera**: Capture and post campus moments instantly with the integrated viewfinder camera.

### 🎬 Duo Dates
Synchronous virtual experiences to break the ice and hang out inside the app.
- **Synchronized Cinema**: Enjoy watch parties together with real-time YouTube video sync and live text/voice chat.
- **Music Date**: Listen to music simultaneously, sharing playlists and vibes in real time.
